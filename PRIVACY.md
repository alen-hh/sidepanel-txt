# Privacy Policy for Sidepanel TXT

Last updated: June 6, 2026

## Overview

Sidepanel TXT is a Chrome extension that allows you to capture and manage quick text snippets directly in your browser's side panel.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to external servers.**

The only data this extension handles is:
- Text that you manually enter into the extension's text input field
- Metadata associated with your text entries (timestamps)

## Data Storage

All your data is stored **locally in your Chrome browser** using Chrome's built-in Storage Sync API. This means:

- Your text snippets are saved directly in your browser
- If you're signed into Chrome and have sync enabled, your data may be synced across your devices through Google's sync service (subject to Google's privacy policy)
- We have no access to your stored data
- Your data never leaves your browser except through Chrome's own sync mechanism

## Permissions

This extension requires the following permissions:

- **storage**: To save your text snippets locally in Chrome's storage
- **sidePanel**: To display the extension interface in Chrome's side panel
- **host_permissions**: Required for extension functionality (no data is collected from visited websites)

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services or analytics platforms.

## User Control

You have complete control over your data:

- You can delete individual text entries using the delete button
- You can remove all extension data by uninstalling the extension from Chrome
- You can disable Chrome sync in your browser settings to prevent data synchronization across devices

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
